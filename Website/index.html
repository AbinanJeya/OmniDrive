Privacy posture

Your files stay with your providers.

OmniDrive does not run a separate cloud backend for your data. It connects from your desktop app, stores OAuth token material through the operating system keyring, and keeps local app state on your machine.

  • Google OAuth is handled by the desktop app.
  • Secrets are persisted through secure OS keyring storage.
  • Preview cache files are app-owned local assets.
  • Transfers and mutations are tracked as visible jobs.
